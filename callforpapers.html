IC@MACE 2023 invites original research articles on topics lying within the scope of the conference. The colloquium will have multiple tracks in various Engineering and Management disciplines. Interested authors are requested to submit an abstract (link), not exceeding 500 words, to brief their research findings. Intimation of acceptance will be sent to the corresponding author through email. Authors of selected articles should submit the full paper in IEEE format (link of sample) with a limit of 5 pages.

Accepted papers will be published in the conference proceedings, provided that at least one of the authors register for the conference.
The colloquium also offers the option to publish selected papers in Scopus indexed International Journals. Kindly note that the journal publication will be subject to the guidelines prescribed by the publisher.

Authors can submit their paper through this link.

Registration

After the submission of full paper, authors are requested to register for the conference through the link. Details of registration fee payable are as given below.

Category Fees
Student Authors/Delegates INR 1500
Authors/Delegates from Academia INR 2000
Authors/Delegates from Industry INR 3000
Journal Publication Fees (if opted for) Will be informed on acceptance based on journal

Mode of payment of registration fees will be informed along with the acceptance mail.
